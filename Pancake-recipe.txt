Ingredients:
 - Whole wheat flour 1cup
 - eggs 2
 - milk 1cup
 - sugar as per sweetness level
 - salt as per taste
 - baking soda a small pinch 
 - butter 2tbsp
 - vanilla or some other essence of your choice

Directions:
 1. In a large bowl mix egg, milk, butter, sugar, salt, vanilla essence and 
 baking powder. Add flour until the mix is smooth and thick batter. 

 2. Place a pan on the stove and spread a table spoon of ghee or butter.

 3. Pour the batter to the pan and wait until the side on the heat 
 turns golden brown.

 4. Add honey or chocolate syrup on top of the pancake and ready to serve. :)
